Lion and Rat

In a dense forest, a mighty lion roamed, feared by all creatures. One day, after a satisfying hunt, the lion lay down for a nap beneath a shady tree. Nearby, a tiny rat scurried about, accidentally waking the lion by running across his paw. The lion roared, his paw pinning the trembling rat. "How dare you disturb me! Why shouldn't I eat you?" The rat, though terrified, pleaded, "Spare me, and someday I might help you in return." Amused, the lion laughed, "How could someone as small as you ever help me?" But he let the rat go.
